## Purpose

This brief is the **only artifact** used to promote a project from ideation (KV/Project Genesis) into FORGE. It captures intent, scope, and authority before any specification work begins.

**Who approves this:** Leo (Human Lead)  
**Who receives this:** CP (Spec Author)
